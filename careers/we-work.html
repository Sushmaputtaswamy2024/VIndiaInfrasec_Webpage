Beware of fake job offers

VIndia does not appoint agents to recruit on behalf of the company. It appears that fake offers / interview call letters indicating the name of VIndia Infratech or deceptively similar name(s) are being circulated by some unknown persons asking for payment of money / security deposit against fake job offers. VIndia Infratech Pvt Ltd shall not be responsible for any such fraudulent / unauthorized activity or the consequences of any action taken on such fraudulent communication.

VIndia advises all concerned not to respond to any such fraudulent job offers and not to make any payments. In case of any doubt/clarifications, candidates are advised to write to info@vindiainfrasec.com .
