About VIndia Infrasec

VIndia, which started off as a small villa project in 2010, has today defied all odds to be recognized as a spearheading force of engineering construction in South India. It started its operations in the form of a partnership firm in the year 2010. In the year 2020, it got incorporated as a Private Limited Company.

It is now one of the fastest-growing construction and manpower-sourcing companies including construction management, consulting services, and other related services, with modern information technology systems and equipment for effective planning, budgeting, estimating, execution, and monitoring of all projects.

We believe in adopting innovative technologies, benchmark quality, robust design/engineering, and uncompromising business ethics. With high levels of dynamism and perseverance, the company has developed expertise in implementing challenging and complex infrastructure projects.

With a reputation for being a company that settles for nothing but the best, VIndia has carved a niche for itself as one of the major players in the industry. VIndia, headquartered in Bangalore, has a strong presence across South India and is rapidly expanding in key locations.
