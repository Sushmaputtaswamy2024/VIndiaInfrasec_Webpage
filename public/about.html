About VIndia Infrasec

Building trust, quality, and innovation since 2010 — delivering civil construction, interiors, MEP, structural design, and project management services across South India.

VIndia, which started as a small villa project in 2010, has today grown into one of South India’s most trusted engineering & construction companies. What began as a partnership firm has now transformed into a strong and dynamic Private Limited Company (since 2020).

We specialise in turnkey construction, interior execution, project management, manpower solutions, and technical consulting — supported by modern systems for planning, budgeting, estimation, scheduling, and progress monitoring.

Our philosophy is built on innovation, attention to detail, quality craftsmanship, and ethical execution. With experienced architects, engineers, designers, and on-site teams, we deliver projects that are robust, functional, and aesthetically refined.

Headquartered in Bangalore, VIndia has established a strong presence across Karnataka, Kerala, Tamil Nadu & other key regions — with a growing portfolio of residential, commercial, and industrial projects delivered with precision and commitment.
