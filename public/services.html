Our Services

From construction consulting to pest management and security services, VIndia Infrasec delivers a complete range of solutions for residential, commercial and industrial projects.

Construction Consultant

VIndia Infrasec ensures the best outcome for your project with experienced construction professionals providing leadership, effective planning, budgeting, execution and monitoring. From site analysis to seismic zone considerations, groundwater information, sewage and future development studies, we guide you at every stage of the project lifecycle.

Manpower Services

We provide reliable manpower services including skilled and unskilled labour for various construction and facility operations. Our manpower is trained to deliver productivity, quality workmanship and to maintain a cooperative and safe work environment at your sites.

General Contracting

As a Class-A general contractor, VIndia Infrasec offers end-to-end construction services. Our approach combines the attention to detail of high-end residential work with the systems and professionalism of commercial construction, delivering superior quality, cost control and on-time execution for projects of all sizes.

Construction Cost Consultant

We provide detailed cost management and quantity surveying services: BOQ preparation, estimates, cost planning, value engineering, budget monitoring and more. Our experts help you control cost without compromising quality across civil, finishing, MEP and infrastructure works.

Water Pipeline Work

We execute water pipeline projects including excavation, trenchless laying, duct installation and water/wastewater connections. Our team plans and implements pipeline solutions considering terrain, regulatory norms, environmental aspects and long-term performance.

Hospitality Services

We offer comprehensive hospitality services including cleaning, floor treatment, carpet and upholstery shampooing and solution-based maintenance for hotels, offices and commercial spaces, ensuring a hygienic, presentable environment.

Pest Management Services

Our pest control solutions cover general pests, termites and rodents with tailored treatment plans for homes, apartments, offices and industrial spaces. We focus on long-term prevention and safe application practices.

Remodeling Services

From concept to completion, our remodeling team handles layout changes, interior upgrades, finishes and services to transform existing homes or commercial spaces into modern, efficient and aesthetically pleasing environments.

Painting

We provide quality painting services for residential and commercial properties using proper surface preparation, material selection and application techniques for durable, clean finishes.

Landscaping

We design and develop outdoor spaces including softscapes, hardscapes and garden features to create functional, sustainable and beautiful landscapes for villas, apartments and commercial campuses.

Security Service

VIndia Infrasec provides trained security personnel and security management services for residential communities, commercial complexes and industrial premises, focusing on safety, vigilance and professional conduct.

Corona Care & Sanitization

We offer professional disinfection and sanitization services for homes, offices, schools and commercial spaces using appropriate disinfectants and protocols as per government guidelines, with trained staff and PPE.
